# App Store Optimization (ASO) Strategy

## 1. Keywords Strategy
**Primary Keywords**:
- Productivity planner
- Habit tracker
- Daily journal
- Goal setter
- Offline planner

**Secondary Keywords**:
- Time blocking
- Routine builder
- Mental health journal
- Streaks
- ADHD planner (Niche target)

## 2. Competitor Positioning
- **Strengths vs Competitors**: Unlike competitors that split apps (one for habits, one for notes, one for tasks), LifeOS unifies them into a single Offline-First architecture. 
- **Value Proposition**: "One app. Total focus."

## 3. Target Audience
- **Demographics**: Professionals, Students, Entrepreneurs (Age 18-35).
- **Psychographics**: Individuals overwhelmed by context-switching between 5 different productivity apps. Highly values privacy and offline data ownership.

## 4. Future Localization
- To maximize global reach, metadata and screenshots should eventually be localized into:
  - Spanish (es-ES, es-MX)
  - French (fr-FR)
  - German (de-DE)
  - Japanese (ja-JP)
