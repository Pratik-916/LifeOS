# Brand Voice Guide

## Tone & Personality
LifeOS is **calm, focused, and encouraging**. 
We speak to users as a supportive productivity coach, avoiding overly energetic or stress-inducing language.

- **Do**: "You've got a busy day ahead. Let's tackle it."
- **Don't**: "HURRY UP! 15 tasks overdue! 🔥🔥🔥"

## Writing Style & Vocabulary
- **Concise**: Always prioritize brevity. Use active voice.
- **Vocabulary**: Use neutral, professional terms (`Tasks`, `Habits`, `Journal`, `Dashboard`). Avoid gamified or childish terms (no `Quests`, `Loot`, or `Diaries`).

## In-App Copy
- **Empty States**: Must be actionable and warm.
  - *Example*: "No tasks scheduled for today. Enjoy the rest, or get ahead of tomorrow."
- **Error Messages**: Always take the blame and offer a solution.
  - *Example*: "We couldn't reach the server right now. Your changes are saved offline and will sync automatically."
- **Push Notifications**: Brief and highly contextual.
  - *Example*: "Time to wind down. Your evening reflection is ready."

## Marketing Language
- **Focus**: The value proposition is *consolidation* and *privacy*.
- **Tagline**: "One app. Total focus."
- **Keywords**: Offline-first, Unified Productivity, Habit Tracking, Mental Wellness.
