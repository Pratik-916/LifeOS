# Brand Identity Guidelines

LifeOS leverages a clean, minimalist, and productivity-focused aesthetic. Our brand identity conveys focus, calm, and achievement.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Accent**: Indigo / Blue (`#4F46E5` / `bg-indigo-600`) - Used for primary actions, CTA buttons, and active states.
- **Secondary Accent**: Emerald / Green (`#10B981`) - Used for success states, completed habits, and positive reinforcement.
- **Background (Light Mode)**: Clean White (`#FFFFFF`) with subtle gray surface layers (`#F9FAFB`).
- **Background (Dark Mode)**: Deep Gray/Black (`#111827` or `#0F172A`) to reduce eye strain during late-night journaling.
- **Text (Primary)**: Dark Gray (`#1F2937`) for high contrast readability.

## Typography Hierarchy
- **Font Family**: Inter (or system-default sans-serif, e.g., San Francisco on iOS, Roboto on Android).
- **Headings (H1/H2)**: Bold, tight letter spacing, high contrast.
- **Body**: Regular weight, optimal line height for reading long-form journal entries.

## Iconography & Illustration Style
- **Icons**: Outlined, stroke-based icons (e.g., Lucide or Heroicons). Consistent stroke width (2px).
- **Style**: Minimalist, no unnecessary gradients or drop shadows. Flat and intentional.
- **Visual Tone**: Professional but welcoming. The interface should feel like a premium tool, not a toy.
