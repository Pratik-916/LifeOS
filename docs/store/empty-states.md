# Empty State Design Language

Empty states must transform a lack of data into an encouraging onboarding opportunity.

## 1. Planner
- **Headline**: "A blank slate."
- **Supporting Text**: "You have no tasks scheduled for today. Add one below to get started."
- **CTA**: "+ Add Task" (Indigo Primary Button)
- **Desired Emotion**: Calm, unburdened.
- **Illustration**: A clean desk or a checklist with a coffee cup.

## 2. Habits
- **Headline**: "Build your first routine."
- **Supporting Text**: "Consistency is key. What habit do you want to build today?"
- **CTA**: "+ Create Habit"
- **Desired Emotion**: Motivated, capable.
- **Illustration**: A small seedling or stepping stones.

## 3. Goals
- **Headline**: "Dream big."
- **Supporting Text**: "Goals turn your daily habits into long-term achievements."
- **CTA**: "+ Set a Goal"
- **Desired Emotion**: Inspired.
- **Illustration**: A mountain peak or a telescope.

## 4. Journal
- **Headline**: "Your private space."
- **Supporting Text**: "Take a moment to reflect. What's on your mind today?"
- **CTA**: "Start Writing"
- **Desired Emotion**: Safe, reflective.
- **Illustration**: A closed notebook or a comfortable chair.

## 5. Journey (Timeline)
- **Headline**: "The story so far."
- **Supporting Text**: "As you complete tasks and write entries, your life's timeline will build here."
- **CTA**: None (Driven automatically by other actions).
- **Desired Emotion**: Anticipation.
- **Illustration**: A winding road or a photo album.

## 6. Analytics
- **Headline**: "Not enough data yet."
- **Supporting Text**: "Check back after completing a few habits and tasks to see your insights."
- **CTA**: None.
- **Desired Emotion**: Informative.
- **Illustration**: A faint, dotted chart line waiting to be filled.
