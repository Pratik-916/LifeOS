# Logo Design System

## Logo Variants
- **Primary Logo**: Indigo icon with "LifeOS" wordmark in dark gray (Light Mode) or white (Dark Mode).
- **Secondary Logo**: Horizontal layout for tight navbars.
- **App Icon**: Just the isolated Indigo icon (no wordmark) on a solid background.
- **Monochrome Logo**: Single color (solid black or solid white) for Android 13 themed icons and subtle watermarks.

## Technical Requirements
- **Minimum Size**: 16x16px for favicon; 24x24px for App UI. Wordmark should never be rendered smaller than 60px wide to maintain legibility.
- **Clear Space**: A padding equivalent to the height of the "L" in LifeOS must surround the logo at all times.
- **Safe Area**: For the mobile App Icon, the focal glyph must remain within the center 80% to avoid clipping by Apple/Google rounded masking.

## Incorrect Usage (DO NOT)
- **Do not stretch or distort** the aspect ratio.
- **Do not rotate** the logo.
- **Do not change colors** outside of the approved Indigo/Emerald/Monochrome palette.
- **Do not place on low-contrast backgrounds** (e.g., Indigo logo on a dark blue background). Use the white monochrome variant instead.
