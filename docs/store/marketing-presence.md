# Marketing Presence

This document outlines the required external touchpoints needed to support the LifeOS application ecosystem.

## 1. Website Ecosystem (lifeos.io)
- **Landing Page**: A simple, high-conversion page showcasing the 5 primary screenshots, value proposition, and App Store / Play Store download badges.
- **Features Page**: Deep dive into the Offline Sync Engine and Privacy guarantees.
- **Pricing**: Clear tier breakdown (if applicable).

## 2. Support & Legal
- **Help Center / FAQ**: Standard documentation covering "How to Sync", "Resetting Passwords", and "Managing Habits".
- **Contact**: A clear support funnel (`support@lifeos.io`).
- **Privacy Policy**: A dedicated page explicitly detailing our offline-first approach and data encryption (`https://lifeos.io/privacy`).
- **Terms of Service**: Standard EULA (`https://lifeos.io/terms`).

## 3. Community (Future)
- **Social Links**: Twitter/X, Discord, or Reddit links for community building.
- **Changelog**: A public changelog demonstrating active development.
